Belize Christmas 2026
3 families · 8 adults · 8 kids · Dec 23 – Jan 1 · 10 nights
🌴 Jungle 🐠 Reef 🏛 Mayan ruins 🦇 ATM Cave 🏄 Snorkeling
Base 1 — Cayo District · 6 nights
Blancaneaux Lodge (Coppola)
Dec 23–28 · Mountain Pine Ridge · +1 866-356-5881
Dec 23
Arrive · Settle in
Fly SAN → Belize City via Houston/Dallas. Transfer to Blancaneaux (~2hrs). Welcome dinner by the river.
Dec 24
Big Rock Falls + Christmas Eve
4-mile hike to waterfall swimming hole — all ages. Candlelit Christmas Eve dinner at lodge.
Dec 25
Caracol Mayan Ruins
Largest Maya site in Belize. Half-day trip, spider monkeys, Rio Frio Cave en route. Christmas Day lunch back at lodge.
Dec 26
ATM Cave (teens + adults)
National Geographic's #1 cave. Swim in, climb through ceremonial chambers. Ages 8+ required. Younger kids stay at resort with one parent.
Dec 27
Xunantunich Ruins
Hand-cranked river ferry, climb El Castillo pyramid — views into Guatemala. All ages including 4-year-old.
Dec 28
Transfer to Ambergris Caye
45-min charter flight Belize City → San Pedro. Check in to Victoria House.
Base 2 — Ambergris Caye · 4 nights
Victoria House Resort & Spa
Dec 28–Jan 1 · victoria-house.com · +1 800-247-5159
Dec 28
Arrive · Beach afternoon
Check in, settle into resort, afternoon on the dock and beach. Sunset dinner.
Dec 29
Belize Barrier Reef Snorkeling
Full-day reef excursion from resort dock. Hol Chan Marine Reserve + Shark Ray Alley. All ages.
Dec 30
Cave Tubing + San Ignacio
Optional day trip — cave tubing on Caves Branch River. Or free beach day.
Dec 31
New Year's Eve
Beach BBQ, fireworks over the water, kids and adults ringing in the new year together in the Caribbean.
Jan 1
Fly home
Charter back to Belize City, connect through Houston/Dallas → SAN, ORD, DEN.
Japan — Christmas & New Year 2026
O'Donovan family · 15 people · Dec 21 – Dec 31 · 11 days including travel
✈️ JAL JL65 direct SAN→NRT 🗼 3 nights Tokyo ⛷ 5 nights Niseko 🌨 Powder skiing ♨️ Private onsen
Tokyo — Dec 22–25
Dec 21
Fly SAN → Tokyo (JAL JL65)
Departs SAN 11:45 AM. Boeing 787-8. ~12 hours nonstop.
Dec 22
Arrive Narita · Check in
Arrive 4:40 PM. Transfer to Tokyo (Shinkansen or bus ~60 min). Early dinner, rest.
Dec 23
Tokyo Day 1 — Asakusa & Shibuya
Senso-ji Temple, Nakamise shopping street, Shibuya crossing, Harajuku for the kids. Ramen dinner.
Dec 24
Tokyo Day 2 — Shinjuku & TeamLab
TeamLab Planets (book ahead — sells out). Shinjuku for dinner. Christmas Eve in Tokyo is magical — illuminations everywhere.
Dec 25
Fly Tokyo → Sapporo → Niseko
ANA or JAL SFO~55 min flight. Transfer to Niseko by bus or private van (~2 hrs).
Niseko — Dec 25–31
Private chalet · 15 guests · nisekoblack.com · private onsen · ski-in/ski-out
Dec 25
Arrive Niseko · Christmas dinner
Check into private chalet. Private chef Christmas dinner. First look at the mountain.
Dec 26–27
Powder days
Niseko gets the best powder in the world — Hokkaido's Japow is legendary. All ages on the mountain.
Dec 28
Ski + Onsen day
Morning ski, afternoon private onsen at chalet. Evening shabu-shabu dinner in Hirafu village.
Dec 29–30
Free ski days
Explore Grand Hirafu, Hanazono, and Annupuri. Ramen lunches on mountain. Kids ski school available.
Dec 31
New Year's Eve · Fly home
Morning ski, transfer to Sapporo, fly NRT. JAL JL66 NRT→SAN departs evening, lands same day Dec 31 (date line).
