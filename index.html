Belize Christmas 2026
3 families · 8 adults · 8 kids · Dec 23 – Jan 1 · 10 nights
🌴 Jungle🐠 Reef🏛 Mayan ruins🦇 ATM Cave🏄 Snorkeling
Base 1 — Cayo District · 6 nights
Blancaneaux Lodge (Coppola)
Dec 23–28 · Mountain Pine Ridge · +1 866-356-5881
Dec 23
Arrive · Settle in
Fly SAN → Belize City via Houston/Dallas. Transfer ~2 hrs. Welcome dinner by the river.
Dec 24
Big Rock Falls + Christmas Eve
4-mile hike to waterfall swimming hole — all ages. Candlelit Christmas Eve dinner at lodge.
Dec 25
Caracol Mayan Ruins
Largest Maya site in Belize. Spider monkeys, Rio Frio Cave en route. Christmas lunch at lodge.
Dec 26
ATM Cave (teens + adults)
National Geographic's #1 cave. Ages 8+ required. Younger kids stay at resort.
Dec 27
Xunantunich Ruins
Hand-cranked river ferry, climb El Castillo — views into Guatemala. All ages.
Dec 28
Transfer to Ambergris Caye
45-min charter flight Belize City → San Pedro. Check in Victoria House.
Base 2 — Ambergris Caye · 4 nights
Victoria House Resort & Spa
Dec 28–Jan 1 · victoria-house.com · +1 800-247-5159
Dec 28
Arrive · Beach afternoon
Check in, afternoon on dock and beach. Sunset dinner.
Dec 29
Belize Barrier Reef Snorkeling
Full-day reef excursion. Hol Chan Marine Reserve + Shark Ray Alley. All ages.
Dec 30
Cave Tubing or Free Beach Day
Optional cave tubing on Caves Branch River, or a relaxed resort day.
Dec 31
New Year's Eve 🎆
Beach BBQ, fireworks over the water, ring in the new year in the Caribbean.
Jan 1
Fly home
Charter to Belize City, connect through Houston/Dallas → SAN, ORD, DEN.
Japan — Christmas & New Year 2026
O'Donovan family · 15 people · Dec 21–31 · 11 days including travel

Tokyo — Dec 22–25
Dec 21
Fly SAN → Tokyo (JAL JL65)
Departs SAN 11:45 AM. Boeing 787-8. ~12 hrs nonstop.
Dec 22
Arrive Narita · Check in
Arrive 4:40 PM. Shinkansen or bus to Tokyo (~60 min). Early dinner, rest.

Tokyo — Asakusa & Shibuya
Senso-ji Temple, Nakamise shopping, Shibuya crossing, Harajuku. Ramen dinner.
Dec 24
Tokyo — TeamLab & Shinjuku
TeamLab Planets (book ahead). Shinjuku dinner. Christmas Eve illuminations everywhere.
Dec 25
Fly Tokyo → Sapporo → Niseko
ANA/JAL ~55 min flight. Private van transfer to Niseko (~2 hrs).

Dec 25
Arrive · Christmas dinner
Check into private chalet. Private chef Christmas dinner. First look at the mountain.
Dec 26–27
Powder days

Free ski days
